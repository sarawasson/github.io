Dr Sara-Patricia Wasson

writing · literature · clarity

I work with story, structure, suffering, and hope. Researching across critical medical humanities and environmental humanities, I study how human and other-than-human worlds meet in illness and recovery, in plant histories and cultural narratives, and in the ways language makes space and silence for complex experience.

Gothic Science Fiction (Liverpool University Press, co-edited with Emily Alder).

Translating Pain: Flash Anthology

Translating Pain: Moments and Fragments — an international online anthology of flash writing on pain experience. Funded by the AHRC as part of the Translating Chronic Pain project, for which I was Principal Investigator. Submissions welcome!
